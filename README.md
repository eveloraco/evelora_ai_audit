## What This Is

Most organisations think they are further along with AI than they actually are.

This tool was built to close that gap. The AI Readiness Audit asks 10 honest questions across 5 categories that actually matter — not whether you have heard of ChatGPT, but whether your leadership uses AI personally, whether your data is clean enough for AI to work with, and whether you have shipped anything real in the last 12 months.

The result is a score out of 100, a radar chart breakdown, and 3 personalised recommendations targeting exactly where your organisation is weakest.

No login. No data stored. Completely free.

| Output | What It Tells You |

## The 5 Categories

| Category | Weight | What It Measures |
|---|---|---|
| Current Usage | 25 pts | How much of the team actually uses AI daily |
| Leadership Adoption | 20 pts | Whether leadership uses AI personally |
| Data Infrastructure | 20 pts | Whether data is clean enough for AI to work with |
| Strategy & Investment | 20 pts | Whether there is a real AI plan or just talk |
| Execution History | 15 pts | Whether real AI projects have actually shipped |

---

## The 5 Tiers

| Score | Tier | What It Means |
|---|---|---|
| 0 – 20 | AI Unaware | Not started. Urgency is high. |
| 21 – 40 | AI Curious | Awareness is there. Execution is missing. |
| 41 – 60 | AI Developing | Real moves made. The gap now is system, not intent. |
| 61 – 80 | AI Ready | Strong foundation. Ahead of most. Ready to scale. |
| 81 – 100 | AI Leader | Genuinely embedded. Top tier globally. |

---

## The Scoring Model

```
Total Score = Current Usage      (max 25)
            + Leadership Adoption (max 20)
            + Data Infrastructure (max 20)
            + Strategy            (max 20)
            + Execution History   (max 15)
            ─────────────────────────────
            = Score out of 100
```

Recommendations are generated dynamically based on the 3 lowest-scoring categories. Each recommendation is personalised to whether the score is in the low or high half of that category's range — so no two results are identical.

## Why This Exists

While building the AI Adoption Index, one finding kept coming back: the gap between how AI-ready organisations think they are and how AI-ready they actually are is significant and consistent.

The executives approving AI budgets use AI at 18% personally. Agriculture — the sector every conference says AI will revolutionise — sits at 42% adoption. The narrative and the data do not match.

This tool was built to give founders and executives an honest, fast, personalised answer to a question most of them have never actually asked themselves with real scrutiny: how ready are we?

---

## The Methodology

All scoring weights were derived from the AI Adoption Index research — cross-referencing McKinsey's Global AI Survey, IBM's AI Adoption Index 2024, and Stanford HAI AI Index 2025 findings on what factors most strongly correlate with successful AI integration at the organisational level.

No synthetic data. No guesswork. Every weight is grounded in the research.
